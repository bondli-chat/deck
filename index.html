The Solution
AI-first moderation
  • Every message processed instantly by AI
  • Only flagged for human review when it actually matters
  • Consistent, unbiased, fully auditable
Grant-ready behavioral data
  • AI moderation generates structured, anonymized behavioral data as a byproduct
  • Wardens use it to back grant applications — recidivism reduction, reentry, mental health
  • Communications system becomes a funding asset
Lower costs. More contact.
  • Families paying $500/month ration every message
  • Drop the cost and volume goes up
  • Sustained contact is one of the strongest predictors of successful reentry
  • Lower recidivism = lower long-term per-bed costs
